Lions are powerful and majestic animals known as the kings of the jungle.

About Lions

Lions live mainly in Africa and are known for their strength, teamwork, and loud roar. Male lions have impressive manes that make them look even bigger.

Fun Facts

  • A lion's roar can be heard up to 8 km away
  • Lions live in groups called prides
  • Male lions protect the pride
  • Lions can run up to 80 km/h for short distances
